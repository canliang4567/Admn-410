About Us

Durham House of Pets offers pet supplies that we love as much as our customers do. We believe in supplying our store with as products that are not only amazing for your pets, but also from brands that care about the world we all live in.

Our History

Our store had its grand opening on September 1, 1980. Since our grand opening, we have expanded to be a nation-wide business with regional offices on the West Coast and Florida. Our flagship store, continues in its original location in Durham, where we strive to give back to the community that welcomed us and supported us during our early years.
