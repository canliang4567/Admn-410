Policies

Returns

We offer a 14 day return policy. Simply bring the product back to our store and we will be happy to offer you a refund or product exchange.

Financing

We are happy to take credit, or cash payments. At this time, we do not have any in-store financing.

Shipping

We are a partner with FedEx and offer next day, and ground shipping within the United States. International Shipping is calculated at time of purchase.
